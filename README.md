# VFS Regular Savings Plan Illustration Calculator

A standalone web-based regular savings plan illustration calculator for VFS client review.

The calculator lets an adviser model:

- Initial investment
- Regular monthly, quarterly, or annual contributions
- GBP, USD, or EUR currency display
- Low, medium, and high annual growth assumptions
- Contribution charge and annual management charge
- 5, 10, 15, or 20 year time frames
- Downloadable PDF illustration for client discussions

Open `index.html` in a browser to use the VFS calculator.
